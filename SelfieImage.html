What is Self Image?

Self image is the way how you and other see yourself as a person. Another word for self image is self esteem. When you have good self esteem, this means that.....
1. You feel good about yourself

2. You feel proud and confident of everything you do

3. You believe in yourself, even when you do not succeed at first

Low self-esteem is another way of thinking and feeling about yourself.

People with low self-esteem.......
1. Don't feel good about themselves

2.Don't think they are as good as others

3.Think mostly bad things about themselves

4. Think of the times they fail rather than the times they do well

5.Are hard on themselves and give up easily

6. Don't feel liked, accepted, or respected
